Welcome to the Quiz App

This is a fun and interactive way to test your knowledge on various topics. Get ready to challenge yourself!

How to Play

  1. Click the 'Start' button to begin the quiz.
  2. Choose the number of questions, category, difficulty level, and type of questions.
  3. Try to answer all questions correctly to get a high score.
  4. Don't forget to check the leaderboard to see how you compare with other players.

Benefits of Taking the Quiz

  • Improve your knowledge in various fields.
  • Enhance your problem-solving and critical thinking skills.
  • Boost your memory and cognitive abilities.
  • Have fun while learning new things.

Remember, the goal is to learn and have fun. Good luck!
